Detailed Description

Attempts to abstract the HSBK concept, and allow for easy assignment and management of LIFX specific HSBK colors.

HSBK is Hue, Saturation, Brightness, and Kelvin. It roughly maps to HSV, but includes the Kelvin argument. When assigning the HSBK to a QColor, Kelving is ignored unfortunately. Therefore, it's not possible to map 1-1. I'm still looking into how to "fix" this.
