\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
(P\+R\+I\+V\+A\+TE) The header for all packets sent to/from a bulb. 

This is the L\+I\+FX header and is used internally for communication. Parts may be exposed by the \mbox{\hyperlink{classLifxBulb}{Lifx\+Bulb}} class, but overall, this isn\textquotesingle{}t available for use by applications.
